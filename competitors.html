Our competitors

Xbox Mobile App

Strong

  • Design looks nice, and fits with Xbox.
  • There are a lot of options in the hamburger menu, but they’re all self explanatory, and very distinct.

Weak

  • UI is unresponsive, the animations that are played when selecting a different item.
  • Unable to disable animations.

Playstation Mobile App

Strong

  • Design is bright and fits well with Playstation
  • Bottom menu is clear, you know exactly what you’ll get when you press on a button

Weak

  • Bottom menu is a little overwhelming, there are a lot of options available
  • All actions, beside friends related stuff, send you to an embedded webpage, which shows you the mobile version of the sony store
