Extended Features

  • Authenticate login (Steam Guard)
  • Authenticate purchase (Steam authenticator)
  • Remote download games

We want the steam mobile app to be an extension to the desktop client. The mobile application should have every feature the desktop client has (except from playing games of course) and in addition to that there are some extra features, like Steam authenticator, Steam Guard (to put an extra layer of protection on your account) and the ability to start a download on your pc from the mobile application.
