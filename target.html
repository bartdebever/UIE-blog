Why does the application suit our target users

With this application the user can chat with his or her friends, view statistics and screenshots of games, visit the store to view, review, read about and buy games. Also they can use the app to authenticate actions they do on the Steam Desktop App

Goals

  • People that want to manage and buy their games for PC when not actually at their PC.
  • People that use steam authentication.
  • People that want to stay in contact with their gaming friends and want to share their games and gaming experience with them.

Problems users have reaching their goals

  • UI is unresponsive and unintuitive
  • Too complicated, buttons are in menus you wouldn’t expect
  • On-descriptive names on menus
  • Categories differ from the desktop app
  • Outdated icons
  • UI is boring (only grey and dark colors), can’t be changed
  • (Almost) no language preference

Problems that will be solved by our application

  • Menus will be less complicated and easier to use
  • UI will be more responsive and intuitive
  • Language can be set separately of system-language
  • Names and menus will be updated to represent their functionality and the steam desktop application

Problem categories

  • Learnability
  • Efficiency
  • Memorability
  • Satisfaction
  • Similarities (with Desktop app)
  • Functionality
