Assignment 1

Assignment Link to implementation
Determine your users and, more importantly, their goals Target Audience
Determine what kind of problems your users have to reach their goals. Target Audience
Which problems will be solved by your application? Target Audience
Search for applications which already exist and which cover the same user group (aim for 2 different applications) Competitors
Make a list of strong and weak points in the existing applications. Also consider which usability factor(s) are involved in every weak & strong point. Competitors
Determine the target users for your application and why the application suits them. Target Audience
Observe your target group through job shadowing and contextual interviews (have them work with the existing applications found before). Determine the problems they have in reaching their goals. Focus Test
Create categories for all problems found (combine them into groups, using the usability factors might help). Target Audience
Record the found problems of your target users by taking pictures or making a short video of the job shadowing or interviews. Explain who you observed and how this went. Explain your results. Focus Test
Create personas (one for each group member) that are typical for your application or describe key persons from your user group in the same level of detail as a persona. Explain your choices. Persona
